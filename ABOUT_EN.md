# ABOUT.md — investment-brain v2.0

## What is this?

investment-brain is a **lightweight investment analysis skill** based on six-agent collaborative decision making.

v2.0 is the initial release, providing basic six-brain signal aggregation.

## Core Capabilities

### Six-Agent Decision System
Each agent analyzes independently, then signals are aggregated:
- **ValuationAgent** (30%) — Is valuation cheap? Enough margin of safety?
- **FundamentalsAgent** (25%) — Is fundamentals healthy? ROE/margins/cash flow?
- **TechnicalsAgent** (20%) — What's the technical trend? MA/RSI/MACD?
- **SentimentAgent** (15%) — What's market sentiment? Money flow?
- **RiskManager** (10%) — Is risk controlled? Forensic red flags?

### Methodology Sources
- Buffett & Munger value investing philosophy
- SEIC moat framework (simplified)
- Technical multi-indicator confluence

## Limitations

v2.0 does **NOT** include the following features (these are in new-investment-brain):
- ❌ Chokepoint bottleneck diagnosis
- ❌ Deep moat assessment
- ❌ Three Moments (AJI🍶/RPI🍓/GATE🛡️)
- ❌ 11-person investment committee
- ❌ Serenity Alpha model

## Why Two Separate Skills?

| Skill | Positioning |
|-------|-------------|
| **investment-brain** | Lightweight, quick analysis, daily use |
| **new-investment-brain** | Deep analysis, Chokepoint+moat+committee, research use |

**Recommendation for new users**: Just use **new-investment-brain** — it's more complete and actively maintained.
